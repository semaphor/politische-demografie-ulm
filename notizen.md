## blogdown

Doku, Buch: https://bookdown.org/yihui/blogdown/

Kommandos:
```
install.packages("blogdown")
blogdown::install_hugo()

blogdown::serve_site()
blogdown::stop_server()

blogdown::check_content()
blogdown::check_site()

blogdown::build_site(build_rmd = 'newfile')
blogdown::build_site()
```
